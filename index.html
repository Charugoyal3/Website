Smart Cities

A Smart city is an urban area that uses different types of electronic Internet of Things (IoT) sensors to collect data and then use the insights gained from that data to manage assets, resources and services efficiently.

Industrial IOT

The Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT) in industrial sectors and applications. It brings together machines and processes to monitor, collect, exchange, and analyze real-time sensor data to deliver meaningful insights.
